About Me

I am open to opportunities where I can contribute meaningfully, grow as a developer, and collaborate with others who care about building great software.

Now graduated with a Computer Science degree from Fitchburg State University, I'm focused on building the next chapter of my career as a software developer. My path began with self-taught programming, evolved through formal education, and continues today with a variety of side projects that push my skills further in both practical and creative directions.

Before tech, I worked as an educator—a background that taught me how to learn quickly, communicate clearly, and adapt to new challenges. Those strengths have translated naturally into my work as a developer.

I work primarily in Python and enjoy building tools and web apps using Flask. I’m also continuing to grow my skills in C++. No matter the language or framework, I enjoy solving problems and writing code that makes things simpler and smarter.
